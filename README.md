# ☘️ Gaeltacht Connect — 30 Day Gaeltacht Challenge

**The old words are listening. Will you speak?**

30 real-world challenges to bring Irish into your daily life. Not flashcards. Not grammar drills. Real actions, with real people, in real places.

## What is this?

A web app that gives you one Irish language challenge per day for 30 days. Each challenge takes you out into the real world — ordering coffee, greeting neighbours, commenting on the weather — all as Gaeilge.

**Week 1:** Greetings & basics — Dia dhuit, Go raibh maith agat, Slán  
**Week 2:** Food, drink & shopping — order, ask prices, express preferences  
**Week 3:** People & places — introductions, directions, family, colours  
**Week 4:** Full immersion — music, proverbs, writing, pub, teaching others  

## Features

- 🎯 One challenge per day — focused, not overwhelming
- 📈 Progress tracking with streaks
- 🌙 Light / dark theme
- 📱 Mobile-first design
- 📤 Share your progress as an image
- 💾 Progress saves between sessions
- 🇮🇪 Built in Ireland, for Ireland
